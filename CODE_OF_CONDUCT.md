# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for
everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background or
identity.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
